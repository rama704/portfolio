Graduation Project: Info Green

InfoGreen is a project designed to solve a common challenge faced by university students‚Äîespecially first-year students‚Äîwho struggle to choose the right courses due to limited or inaccurate information. The system provides a reliable platform that helps students select appropriate courses based on prerequisite relationships and their academic study plan. The project successfully achieved its objective by creating a system capable of managing data and assisting students in course selection.
